Hamdan Restaurant

The Hamdan Restaurant chain is a prominent and well-known culinary establishment that offers many types of delicious chicken dishes that distinguish it from other restaurants. It is an institution that contains three branches, including a main branch located in the city and operating 24 hours a day, and two other branches in the governorates. The restaurant chain seeks to provide advanced software solutions to provide the most excellent service to restaurant customers and raise their level of satisfaction.
